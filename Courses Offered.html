Welcome to the Courses Section!

At Sungabha Public School, we are committed to providing a diverse and quality education for our students. We offer a range of courses that cater to different academic interests and career paths. Whether you're interested in Management, Humanities, or Education, our courses are designed to provide a strong foundation for your future success.

Courses Offered:

  • Ten Plus Two (+2) Management - A comprehensive course in business, finance, and management.
  • Ten Plus Two (+2) Humanities - A well-rounded program focusing on literature, social sciences, and humanities.
  • Ten Plus Two (+2) Education - A course designed for those interested in becoming educators and learning about the education system.
