Sungabha Public School Location

Our Location

Sungabha Public School is conveniently located at a prime location in Murgiya, easily accessible by both private and public transport. The school is situated in a peaceful and educationally enriching environment, making it ideal for students to focus on their studies and extracurricular activities.

Public transportation services, including buses and taxis, are available in the vicinity, ensuring hassle-free access for students. The school also provides ample parking space for private vehicles. Whether you're coming from the City center or nearby areas, getting to Sungabha Public School is quick and convenient.

We invite you to visit us and explore the beautiful surroundings of our school/college. The combination of our central location and the tranquil setting makes Sungabha Public School the perfect place for students to grow and thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
