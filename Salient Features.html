Salient Features

Sungabha Public School is proud to offer a diverse range of features that contribute to a holistic education experience for our students. The following features play a crucial role in the development of our students both academically and personally:

  • Library: A well-stocked library that encourages a love for reading and research.
  • Sports: Comprehensive sports facilities to promote physical health, teamwork, and competitive spirit.
  • Science Lab: Fully equipped science laboratories for hands-on learning and experiments.
  • Computer Lab: Modern computer lab with internet access, supporting technological learning and innovation.
  • Multimedia: Multimedia classrooms for a dynamic and interactive learning environment.
  • Counseling: Dedicated counseling services to support studentsâ€™ mental health and career guidance.
  • Scholarships: Scholarships offered to deserving students to support their educational journey.
  • Classrooms: Spacious, well-lit classrooms designed for effective teaching and learning.
  • Conference Hall: A state-of-the-art conference hall for events, presentations, and meetings.
  • Dance & Music: Specialized dance and music programs that foster creativity and self-expression.
  • Tour: Educational tours and trips designed to broaden the students' knowledge and worldview.
  • Extracurricular Activities (ECA): A variety of extracurricular activities to nurture skills beyond academics and encourage all-round development.

Comprehensive Student Development

At Sungabha Public School, we believe in developing well-rounded individuals. We provide a variety of programs aimed at enhancing personal growth, leadership, and social responsibility. Along with a strong academic foundation, students have the opportunity to excel in extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and community service. We are committed to helping our students realize their full potential in every area of life.
