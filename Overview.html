About Sungabha Public School

Sungabha Public School, located in Sainamaina-03, Murgiya, Rupandehi, Nepal, is a prominent educational institution established in 2053 BS (1996 AD). The school offers a comprehensive curriculum from primary to higher secondary levels, providing quality education to students in the region. With a dedication to academic excellence and holistic development, Sungabha prepares students for a successful future.

Mission and Vision

Sungabha Public School is dedicated to fostering a nurturing environment that celebrates curiosity, creativity, and a love for lifelong learning. The institution aims to provide a rigorous academic program that challenges and inspires students, cultivates a supportive and inclusive environment, and equips students with critical thinking skills and knowledge for success in higher education and beyond. We strive to be a school of choice, renowned for its holistic approach to education and its focus on excellence.

Community and Environment

Sungabha Public School is more than just a place to learn; it's a vibrant community where students, teachers, and staff come together to explore, discover, and achieve their full potential. The school encourages collaboration and community, working together to create a positive and inspiring learning environment.

Our school is committed to promoting values such as responsibility, integrity, and teamwork. We believe in shaping well-rounded individuals who are not only academically strong but also socially and emotionally aware. Through engaging in community service, group activities, and interactive learning experiences, students develop a sense of belonging and a drive to contribute positively to society.

We also emphasize environmental awareness and sustainability, encouraging students to be mindful of their surroundings and take action to preserve the planet for future generations.
